Detection Engine

All leaf images are analyzed using a convolutional neural network (CNN) model trained specifically on tomato disease datasets.

CNNs automatically learn visual patterns such as spots, discoloration and texture changes directly from images, making them far more accurate than manual rule-based systems or simple thresholding methods.

This deep-learning approach has become the state-of-the-art for plant disease detection because it is robust to lighting, camera quality and background noise, and it can continually improve as new labeled examples are added.
